Electronics Voting Machine

An Electronic Voting Machine (EVM) is a device used to electronically record votes in an election, replacing traditional paper ballots. It consists of a control unit and a ballot unit, which are designed to securely capture and store votes while preventing tampering or fraud.

Line-Follower Robot

A line-following robot is an autonomous robot that detects and follows a pre-defined path, usually marked by a line on the ground. It uses sensors, typically infrared, to track the line and adjust its movement accordingly.

Gird Forming Inverter

A Grid-Forming Inverter (GFM)is an advanced inverter that operates independently to establish and regulate grid voltage and frequency, enabling stable islanded or weak grid operation. Unlike grid-following inverters, it can synchronize and support grid stability without relying on an external power source.
